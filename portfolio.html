Leaflet FCC / ACS Need Index

Illinois Broadband Gap Analysis

Interactive mapping platform identifying broadband service gaps across 3,263 Illinois census tracts, combining FCC coverage data with poverty and educational attainment into a customizable Need Index for infrastructure planners and grant writers.

Data FCC Broadband Data Collection · ACS 5-Year 2024 · Census TIGER/Line 2023
Method Composite Need Index: 50% Coverage Gap + 30% Poverty + 20% Educational Attainment · User-adjustable weights
Scale 3,263 census tracts · Statewide Illinois · Fixed & mobile broadband layers

GPlates Plate Tectonics 200 Ma

Gulf of Mexico Plate Kinematic Reconstruction

A reconstruction of the Gulf of Mexico basin from 200 million years ago to present using the Müller et al. plate model and GPlates. Covers plate motion, spreading rates, and subsidence — useful context for understanding why the GoM became one of the most productive hydrocarbon basins in the world.

Data: Müller et al. (2019) · Stack: GPlates 2.4, pyGPlates, Leaflet, Plotly
