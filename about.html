About Me

I am a passionate GIS professional currently pursuing my Master's degree in Cartography and Geographic Information Sciences at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. With a strong foundation in geographic information systems and two bachelor's degrees from Michigan State University, I bring together technical expertise and creative problem-solving skills in my work.

My professional experience spans various aspects of GIS — from creating detailed project plan sets at Bono Consulting to designing complex visualizations at Primera Engineers. I'm particularly interested in the intersection of cartography, data visualization, and web-based mapping technologies.

When I'm not working with maps, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source GIS projects, or cycling through Chicago's diverse neighborhoods — an experience that has deepened my appreciation for urban geography and spatial analysis.
