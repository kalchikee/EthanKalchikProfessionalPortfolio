Leaflet EIA / FEMA NRI Risk Index

Power Grid Fragility — Blackout Vulnerability Index

Nationwide composite risk model across all 3,144 US counties, combining grid reliability metrics, weather hazard exposure, elderly population share, and poverty rate into a single weighted vulnerability index.

Data EIA-861/860 · FEMA NRI v1.20 · Census ACS · EIA Outage Records
Method 5-factor weighted composite: 30% Grid + 25% Hazard + 20% FEMA NRI + 15% Elderly + 10% Poverty
Scale 3,144 counties · 50 states · 847 counties rated High or Very High risk

Leaflet OLS Regression ACS / TIGER

Police Response Deserts — Detroit 911 Equity Analysis

Spatial regression analysis testing whether police response times in Detroit correlate with neighborhood race and income, mapping the geography of delayed emergency response across 139 census tracts.

Data Detroit CAD 911 records · Census ACS 2022 · TIGER/Line boundaries
Method OLS regression (response time ~ % non-white + median income) · Spatial join to census tracts
Finding Statistically significant response time disparities correlated with race and income

XGBoost 0.87 AUC-ROC Public Health

The Lead Pipe Map — Chicago Lead Service Line Prediction

XGBoost classification model predicting which of Chicago’s ~800,000 unverified properties are likely connected by a lead service line, using Cook County Assessor records to prioritize infrastructure replacement.

Data Cook County Assessor · Chicago CDOT · Water main age records · Census TIGER/Line
Method XGBoost classification · Top features: construction year, distance to known lead main, water main age
Performance 0.87 AUC-ROC · ~112,000 unverified properties flagged high-risk

Leaflet FCC / ACS Need Index

Illinois Broadband Gap Analysis

Interactive mapping platform identifying broadband service gaps across 3,263 Illinois census tracts, combining FCC coverage data with poverty and educational attainment into a customizable Need Index for infrastructure planners and grant writers.

Data FCC Broadband Data Collection · ACS 5-Year 2024 · Census TIGER/Line 2023
Method Composite Need Index: 50% Coverage Gap + 30% Poverty + 20% Educational Attainment · User-adjustable weights
Scale 3,263 census tracts · Statewide Illinois · Fixed & mobile broadband layers
